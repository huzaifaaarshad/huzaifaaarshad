INTRODUCTION

Le Gavroche was the first UK restruant to be awarded one,two and three Michelin stars. The chef de cuisine is Michel Roux Jr, who took over the reins from his father, Alert in 1991. The face that Le Gavroche continues to maintain the highest reputations amongst both diners and critics is testament to the excellence of the food being prepared by Michel.

OVERVIEW

⦁ Significance of the Project
FOR THE LOVE OF FOOD!!
⦁ Description of the Project
A website based application where customers can view menu items and order them accordingly. A shopping cart will be integrated which will provide customers with various payment options.

METHODOLOGY

⦁ Design phase
The website is designed using basic design principles which make it interactive for users. It also makes it easy to use for users.
⦁ Implementation phase Front-end and back-end technologies have been choosen based on the criteria of their efficiency and wide-spread usage.
⦁ Testing phase
We plan on collecting data from multiple users. We will also rercord their results and work on the bugs that come.
⦁ Evaluation phase
The application will be evaluated by comparing the requirements of the customer with the design and the fulfillment by the application.

FEATURES

⦁ Order.
Customers will be able to see details of all the available dishes.
⦁ Second most significant feature.
Customers will be able to select particular dishes and place order. A notification will be sent to the restruant which will confirm the order. Customers with confirmed orders will be sent a notification.
⦁ Third significant feature.
Interactive UI will enable customers to easily interact with the application.
